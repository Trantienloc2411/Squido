Our Story

Founded in 2010, eDox started as a small bookshop with a big dream: to connect readers with the books they love. What began as a passion project has grown into one of the leading online bookstores, serving customers worldwide.

Our journey has been guided by our love for literature and our commitment to making books accessible to everyone. We believe that books have the power to educate, inspire, and transform lives.

Today, eDox offers millions of titles across various genres, from bestselling fiction to academic textbooks, all at competitive prices and with worldwide shipping.

Our Mission

To provide readers worldwide with access to a diverse collection of books at affordable prices, while delivering exceptional customer service and fostering a love for reading.

Our Vision

To be the world's most customer-centric bookstore, where customers can find and discover anything they might want to read, and where authors can find a global audience.

Why Choose Us

Extensive Collection

Access to millions of books across all genres, including rare and hard-to-find titles.

Fast Shipping

Reliable worldwide shipping with tracking, ensuring your books arrive safely and on time.

Competitive Prices

Best prices on the market, with regular discounts and special offers for our loyal customers.
