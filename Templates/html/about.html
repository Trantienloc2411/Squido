John Smith

Historical Fiction 15 Books 4.8/5

John Smith is an award-winning author of historical fiction and thrillers. Born in Edinburgh, Scotland, he developed a passion for history and storytelling at an early age. After studying History at Oxford University, he worked as a museum curator before turning to writing full-time.

His debut novel, "The Clockmaker's Daughter," won the Historical Fiction Award in 2018, and his books have been translated into 28 languages. Known for his meticulous research and vivid prose, Smith brings historical periods to life with remarkable authenticity and creates characters that resonate with readers long after they've finished his books.

When not writing, John enjoys traveling to historical sites, collecting antique books, and giving lectures on history and the craft of writing. He currently lives in London with his wife and two children.
